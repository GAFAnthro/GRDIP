About the HHRRC

“The American Academy of Forensic Sciences (AAFS) Humanitarian and Human Rights Resource Center (HHRRC) was created in February, 2015. The HHRRC seeks to utilize the assets of the AAFS to promote the application of contemporary forensic science and forensic medicine principles to global humanitarian and/or human rights projects requiring special forensic assistance. The HHRRC also provides support to AAFS members engaged in human rights and/or humanitarian forensic applications and encourages AAFS members to increase their involvement in such matters. With financial assistance from the National Institute of Justice's Forensic Technology Center of Excellence, and the AAFS, the HHRRC provides monetary awards each year to support research projects and on-the-ground efforts applying forensic science to human rights and humanitarian actions.”
